Essential Care

This highly affordable, entry-level option is ideal for smaller homes (up to 4 residents) that need reliable remote support and basic compliance.

  • **Remote IT Support:** Unlimited remote troubleshooting for non-technical issues.
  • **Core Security:** Setup and management of a single, home-wide internet filtering policy.
  • **Expert Advice:** Unbiased guidance on all IT systems and future purchases.

Compliance Plus

This flagship service provides a complete digital safeguarding solution with granular control and proactive management for most homes (up to 8 residents).

  • **Everything in Essential Care,** plus:
  • **Advanced Safeguarding:** Individualized, per-resident internet filtering policies.
  • **Mobile Device Management (MDM):** Implementation and management of MDM on home-owned devices.
  • **Secure Communications:** Setup and support for an encrypted email system using non-profit licenses.
  • **Proactive Management:** Network monitoring and compliance reports for Ofsted evidence portfolios.
